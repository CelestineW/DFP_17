What is the Gospel?

God As Creator, As Ruler

God is the loving ruler of the world
He made the world.
He made us rulers of the world under Him.

The Fall Of Man

We reject the ruler-- God -- by trying to run life our own way without Him.
But we fail to rule ourselves or society of the world

The Punishment for Sin

God won't let us rebel forever
God's punishment for rebellion is death and judgement

The Hope of Jesus Christ

Because of His love, God sent His son into the world: the man Jesus Christ
Jesus always lived under God's rule.
Yet by dying in our place He took our punishment and brought forgiveness

The Resurrection

God raised Jesus to life again as the ruler of the world
Jesus has conquered death, now gives new life, and will return to judge

The Decision

The two ways to live:


Our way-- to reject the ruler, God, and to try to run life our own way, resulting in God's condemnation, death, and judgement.




God's new way-- to submit to Jesus as our ruler and rely on Jesus' death and resurrecton, granting us forgiveness and eternal life
