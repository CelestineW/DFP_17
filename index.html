Vision and Purpose

The CBCM drama team is a community of Christ-centered dramatic artists serving our church, our neighbors, and our world with our God-given creative gifts and talents.

Our mission as a drama ministry is to reach out to the community by lovingly adding value to their lives through entertaining dramas, public testimony, and personal conversations, hopeful and prayerful that the people who we are ministering to will be led into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ.

Our process as a drama ministry involves the spiritual formation of our drama team members in Christian faith and character, equipping them for personal evangelism, and training them in their dramatic abilities. This process is accomplished through discipleship, fellowship, prayer, evangelism training, and drama coaching. We will also encourage fresh creative expression through the writing and developing of new dramas purposed to better fulfill our mission.
